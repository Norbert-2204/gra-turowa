A Warrior specializes in dealing physical damage through powerful attacks, unaffected by the type of magic or elemental affinity. They rely on sheer strength and combat skill rather than magical abilities to defeat their foes.

A Defender specializes in protection, reducing the damage taken by allies or absorbing it entirely on himself. His role is to shield the team from harm, ensuring others can fight without fear of being overwhelmed.

A healer specializes in restoring the health of their allies, using powerful healing abilities to keep the team alive

A Sorcerer wields the power of elemental magic, unleashing devastating attacks using fire, ice, lightning, and magic type spells

A Spellblade combines mastery of fire and ice magic with precision strikes, dealing devastating damage to enemies. They can also inflict weaknesses to fire and ice, amplifying the effectiveness of their elemental attacks.

A Druid harnesses the magic of earth and wind to control the battlefield, while also supporting allies with healing and mana regeneration. Their versatile abilities make them both a powerful spellcaster and a vital support.

An Alchemist excels as a debuffer, applying elemental weaknesses to enemies and reducing their attack power. At the same time, they empower allies by boosting their attack, making them a key tactical asset in any battle.

An Elemental Master commands every type of magic, casting spells that target all enemies simultaneously. Their abilities deal dual-element damage, making them a formidable force against any opposition.
